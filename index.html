Unbeatable Location, Homely Apartments

Larkfield Apartments offers four wonderful properties in an ideal central Richmond location, perfect if you want to leave the car at home.

A 15-minute walk will find you at Richmond Park, a beautiful stretch of the Thames riverside, two wonderful theatres and an array of bars, restaurants and shops. Guests really appreciate the close proximity of the station and high-street, especially after a long day, and the ease of commute to and from central London.

For those who want to venture further afield, Richmond offers superb transport links to surrounding areas of Twickenham, Wimbledon, Hampton, Kensington, and even a direct link to Windsor. Whether your visit is for pleasure, sightseeing or looking to relocate, you will be astounded by the location and beautiful setting of Richmond.

A taste of Richmond

Richmond is renowned for its Royal Parks and impressive stretches of riverside.

Take a walk through London's largest open space, Richmond Park, where herds of red and fallow deer roam free in 2500 acres of hills, woodlands and grassland - well worth a visit during any stay in Richmond.

The Royal Botanic Kew Gardens, a UNESCO World Heritage site, holds the most unique collection of plants in the world. 



Atop of Richmond Hill offers some of the most stunning views of London, overlooking the winding River Thames and stretching countryside below.
