Travel Information

Rail

Main line trains run every few minutes to Central London, Waterloo Station. Journey time is 15 to 20 minutes. The underground station is 5-20 minutes minutes walk away, depending on the accommodation chosen.


Underground

The District Line from Richmond serves all parts of London including Heathrow Airport. Gatwick airport is accessible by underground via Victoria Station, Gatwick Express.


Road

The M4 is accessible at Junction 2, Kew Bridge. The AA316/M3 is just around the corner.


Buses

Available to all destinations from outside Richmond Station, or near Richmond Bridge.


Supermarkets

Supermarkets are a short walk from all the accommodation locations.
